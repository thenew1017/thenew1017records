The Artists

A roster forged in late nights and louder mornings. Discover the specialized sound vectors defining the next global audio wave.

✓ Flagship TalentLIVE

Pooh shiesty

Pooh Shiesty is an American rapper from Memphis, Tennessee. Known for his raw street storytelling, aggressive flow, and chart-topping hits, he quickly became one of the most recognized voices in modern hip-hop. His music blends authentic street experiences with high-energy production, earning millions of streams worldwide.

✓ Flagship TalentLIVE

Foogiano

Foogiano is an American rapper from Greensboro, Georgia and one of the standout artists signed to Gucci Mane's 1017 Records. Known for his gritty lyrics, Southern trap sound, and authentic storytelling, Foogiano quickly built a loyal fanbase through viral releases and powerful collaborations. His music reflects street experiences, determination, and the signature energy that helped establish him as one of the notable voices in modern trap music.

✓ Flagship TalentLIVE

Big Scarr

Real Name: Alexander Woods Big Scarr was an American rapper from Memphis, Tennessee and one of the most recognized artists signed to Gucci Mane's 1017 Records. Known for his aggressive delivery, authentic street storytelling, and powerful Memphis sound, he gained national attention through records such as SoIcyBoyz, Make A Play, and Big Grim Reaper. His music reflected resilience, ambition, and real-life experiences, helping him become one of the standout voices of the new generation of Southern trap music.

✓ Flagship TalentLIVE

Enchanting

Real Name : Channing Nicole Larry Enchanting was an American rapper, singer, and songwriter from Fort Worth, Texas. Signed to Gucci Mane's 1017 Records, she became known for her unique blend of trap, R&B, and melodic rap. Her signature "Trap-N-Blues" sound, emotional storytelling, and powerful vocals helped her build a loyal fanbase worldwide. Through projects like No Luv and Luv Scarred, Enchanting established herself as one of the most distinctive female voices associated with the 1017 movement.

✓ Flagship TalentLIVE

BiC Fizzle

Real Name :Markies Conway BiC Fizzle is an American rapper from Blytheville, Arkansas who rose to prominence after signing with Gucci Mane's The New 1017 and Atlantic Records. Known for his authentic storytelling, Southern trap sound, and relentless work ethic, he quickly gained attention with tracks like Servin, Turnt Shit, and Clark Street Baby. Combining street experiences with modern trap production, BiC Fizzle established himself as one of the standout young artists of the new 1017 generation.

✓ Flagship TalentLIVE

Waka Flocka

American rapper and entrepreneur best known for hit records like Hard in Da Paint, No Hands, Grove St. Party, and O Let’s Do It. Waka Flocka Flame became one of the most influential artists in trap music and gained recognition through Gucci Mane’s 1017 Brick Squad movement.
